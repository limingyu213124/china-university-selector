Frequently Asked Questions

How does the China University Selector work?

Our tool uses a 9-step interactive quiz to match you with the best Chinese universities based on your field of study, budget, academic level, city preferences, and other criteria. The algorithm considers multiple factors to provide personalized recommendations.

Is the China University Selector free to use?

Yes, our university matching tool is completely free. You get personalized recommendations and detailed analysis at no cost. We believe in making quality education guidance accessible to all international students.

What universities are included in the database?

We include top Chinese universities like Tsinghua University, Peking University, Fudan University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Zhejiang University, and many others with comprehensive program information and rankings.

How accurate are the university recommendations?

Our algorithm considers multiple factors including field compatibility, budget, ranking, city preferences, and language requirements to provide highly accurate matches tailored to your specific needs and preferences.

Can I use this tool for both undergraduate and graduate programs?

Yes! Our tool covers both undergraduate and graduate programs. The quiz includes questions about your academic level to ensure we match you with appropriate programs and universities.

Do you include information about scholarships and application deadlines?

Yes, our university database includes information about available scholarships, application deadlines, acceptance rates, and other important details to help you make informed decisions about your education in China.
